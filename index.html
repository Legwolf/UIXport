About me

Hi, I am Pei-Yun (Lu) Chung.

Working with e-commerce companies and startups, I have more than 3 years of experience in UX, UI, and visual design.

I am specializing in human-centered design of user interfaces for websites and applications. With the practical experiences, my enthusiasm, creativity, and skills drive me to further explore the area of emotional design, human-computer Interaction.

I am also proficient at Photography and Painting. When I'm not designing, I am addicted to travel, capture beautiful moments, and write my thoughts on my blog.

Lately I'm looking for a designer position, worldwide.
